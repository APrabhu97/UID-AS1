Sudoku

In this experiment, you will solve mini Sudoku puzzles.

The rules of Sudoku are to fill in all the digits so that no duplicates appear in any row, column or bold-bordered square.

This version of the interface uses the mouse and/or keyboard. To write a number, either cycle through the options by tapping multiple times on the selected cell, or press a number 1-4 on your keyboard.

Press Clear to clear your inputs. Hold Check Status to see if you are on the right path.

Note: All keystrokes and mouse clicks are being logged to understand how users approach the game.
